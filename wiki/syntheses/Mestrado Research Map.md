# Mestrado Research Map

## Summary

- Current vault centers on research methodology, ERP/enterprise integration, and systems engineering.
- [[wiki/concepts/Design Science Research|Design Science Research]] gives artifact-building logic for applied research.
- [[wiki/concepts/Systematic Literature Review Protocol|Systematic Literature Review Protocol]] and [[wiki/concepts/PRISMA Reporting|PRISMA Reporting]] give evidence-review discipline.
- [[wiki/concepts/Enterprise Resource Planning|Enterprise Resource Planning]] and [[wiki/concepts/Enterprise Integration and Interoperability|Enterprise Integration and Interoperability]] define likely application domain.
- [[wiki/concepts/System of Systems Engineering|System of Systems Engineering]] adds vocabulary for distributed, autonomous, evolving socio-technical systems.

## Working Thesis

This source set can support a master's research program where DSR is the methodological frame, systematic review is the evidence-building method, and ERP/enterprise integration is the application domain. Systems engineering and systems-of-systems literature broaden the framing when ERP integration is treated as a distributed socio-technical system rather than a bounded software deployment.

Confidence: medium. The exact research question still needs formulation.

## Strong Connections

- DSR artifact logic plus ERP implementation reference models: artifact can be a method, model, framework, or instantiation for implementation/integration problems.
- SLR protocol plus PRISMA reporting: protocol governs conduct; PRISMA governs transparent reporting.
- Enterprise modelling plus ERP implementation: multi-view models externalize goals, rules, actors, processes, concepts, and technical components.
- SoS concepts plus enterprise integration: autonomy, independence, interoperability, emergence, and evolution map onto multi-organization enterprise systems.

## Candidate Research Directions

- DSR artifact for ERP implementation governance using enterprise modelling and CSF evidence.
- Systematic review of socio-technical factors in ERP implementation and post-go-live performance.
- Interoperability framework for ERP-centered heterogeneous data integration.
- Systems-of-systems lens for ERP ecosystems across suppliers, consultants, customers, and platforms.
