# Persistent Synthesis

## Summary

Persistent synthesis means insights are stored as durable wiki pages and revised over time, rather than generated once in chat and lost.

## Why It Matters

- Cross-source connections remain available for later questions.
- Contradictions can be tracked explicitly.
- Summaries improve as new evidence arrives.
- Query answers can become new source-like artifacts inside the wiki.

## Maintenance Burden

Humans often abandon wikis because bookkeeping is tedious. Agent maintenance reduces cost of updating links, indexes, summaries, contradictions, and open questions.
