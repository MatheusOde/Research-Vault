# Al-Amin et al. 2023 - ERP in Industry 4.0

## Summary
- State-of-the-art review of ERP history, future technologies, CSFs, and cybersecurity in Industry 4.0.
- Traces ERP from MRP, MRP II, CIM, ERP, ERP II, and cloud ERP.
- Future ERP technologies discussed: cloud, mobile, AI, BI, IoT/IIoT, blockchain, big data, omnichannel strategy, and automation.
- Lists recurring CSFs: top management support, project team, IT infrastructure, communication, skilled staff, training/education, monitoring/evaluation.
- Identifies cybersecurity, confidentiality, cloud risk, heterogeneity, privacy, and scale as major future ERP challenges.

## Details
This source is useful as a broad trend map rather than a narrow empirical paper. It frames cloud ERP as lower cost and easier for SMEs, mobile ERP as access and real-time visibility, AI as forecasting/decision support, BI as analytics, IoT as real-time sensing, blockchain as traceability/compliance, and big data as external plus transactional analytics integration.
