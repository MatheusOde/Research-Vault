# Projeto de Pesquisa Modelo FAPESP

## Summary

- Draft research proposal titled "Integração de sistemas de informação na classe de software ERP".
- Central problem: sociotechnical alignment in systems integration, especially between executive and IT teams with different priorities, languages, and decision criteria.
- Proposed artifact: a prescriptive method based on For Enterprise Modeling (4EM) to support alignment in systems integration projects.
- ERP is used as empirical reference context for demonstration and preliminary evaluation, but the intended problem class is broader than ERP implementation.
- Methodological frame: [[wiki/concepts/Design Science Research|Design Science Research]], supported by a systematic literature review and evaluated through proof of concept plus academic expert panel.

## Core Research Structure

Research question:

> Como um método prescritivo baseado no For Enterprise Modeling (4EM) pode favorecer o alinhamento sociotécnico em projetos de integração de sistemas em organizações, tomando ERP como contexto empírico de referência para demonstração e avaliação preliminar?

General objective:

- Develop and evaluate a prescriptive 4EM-based method for strategic and sociotechnical alignment in organizational systems integration projects, using ERP as reference context.

Specific objectives:

- Analyze sociotechnical alignment problems between executive and IT teams in systems integration projects.
- Formalize the problem class and derive methodological requirements using 4EM dimensions.
- Propose a prescriptive method based on 4EM models.
- Demonstrate and preliminarily evaluate method utility in an ERP-related context.

## Method And Artifact

The draft positions the method as the central DSR artifact. The planned web prototype is an instantiation that operationalizes the method, not the primary contribution.

The method is expected to structure six 4EM perspectives:

- Goals.
- Business rules.
- Processes.
- Actors and resources.
- Concepts.
- Technical components and requirements.

Expected outputs include integrated models, critical dependencies, sociotechnical misalignments, decision criteria, information gaps, responsibilities, and guidance for integration decisions.

## Work Plan

- March: consolidate research map, question, objectives, problem class, and DSR framing.
- April: conduct systematic literature review guided by PRISMA 2020 and start problem-class formalization.
- May: derive requirements, design method version 0.1, and develop web prototype.
- June: run ERP proof of concept, academic panel, method refinement, and partial-result consolidation.

## Evaluation Logic

Evaluation is preliminary and qualitative. It combines:

- Proof of concept in a bounded ERP scenario.
- Analysis of records and visualizations produced by the prototype.
- Academic expert panel with four to six specialists.
- Requirement-evidence-judgment matrix.

Evaluation criteria include clarity, internal coherence, utility, applicability, sociotechnical completeness, traceability, decision support, contextual transferability, and prototype adequacy as method instantiation.
