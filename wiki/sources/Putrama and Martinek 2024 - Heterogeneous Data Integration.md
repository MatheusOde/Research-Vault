# Putrama and Martinek 2024 - Heterogeneous Data Integration

## Summary
- Review of heterogeneous data integration methodologies and approaches.
- Distinguishes physical integration from virtual integration: physical has better query processing but higher cost/maintenance and freshness issues; virtual is flexible and avoids replication but is complex under heterogeneity.
- Finds most reviewed studies focus on big data generally rather than specific domains.
- Finds semantics-related integration dominates the literature, with graph and ontology approaches most common.
- Open gaps include semantic issues, unstructured data, ontology evolution, scalability, privacy, and integrating machine learning with data integration.

## Details
The paper reviews 58 articles from 2012-2023. Research focus categories include semantics-related integration, physical data integration/issues/challenges, and big data integration. Approaches include ETL, wrappers, mediator/wrapper architecture, distributed/federated databases, ontology/schema mapping, graph/RDF, probabilistic semantic association, and ML-based integration.
