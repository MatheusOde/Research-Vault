# Sandkuhl 2017 - Enterprise Modeling

## Summary
- Lecture slide deck introducing enterprise modelling definitions, purposes, analysis techniques, and selected methods.
- Defines enterprise modelling as capturing relevant structures, processes, and knowledge of an enterprise in computerized models for a problem at hand.
- Distinguishes enterprise modelling from process modelling through multiple perspectives: organization, process, subject of work/product, and platform.
- Presents 4EM as a successor of EKD with goal, process, actors/resources, concepts, business rules, and technical components models.
- Emphasizes participatory modelling, management support, scope clarity, stakeholder involvement, and iterative rework.

## Details
Useful concepts for the ERP vault include: model/reality distinction; modelling layers and perspectives; analysis techniques such as interviews, questionnaires, facilitated modelling, observation, document/database/log analysis; and the need to involve the right people and model the right things at the right granularity.
