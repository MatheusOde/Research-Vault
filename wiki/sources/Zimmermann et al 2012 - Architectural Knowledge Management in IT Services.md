# Zimmermann et al. 2012 - Architectural Knowledge Management in IT Services

## Summary
- Presents a reference architecture, decision process-oriented metamodel, tool implementation, and modelling principles for architectural knowledge management in IT services.
- Targets enterprise application development, package integration, cloud computing, and strategic outsourcing proposal work.
- Frames design work as many interdependent decision points under uncertainty, incomplete input, reuse needs, and review requirements.
- Introduces decision knowledge processing with decision point graphs, options, rationale, confidence, undecide/revisit ability, and report/review support.
- Not ERP-specific, but highly reusable for capturing ERP architecture and integration decisions.

## Details
The paper identifies challenges: scope/scale, priority/order, data quality/uncertainty, consistency/efficiency, and reuse/education. Its architecture includes user-facing decision editors/clients, decision point services, a decision knowledge domain model and processing engine, integration middleware, reporting, and backend decision knowledge bases.
