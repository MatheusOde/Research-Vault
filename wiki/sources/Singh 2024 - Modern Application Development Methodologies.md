# Singh 2024 - Modern Application Development Methodologies

## Summary
- Surveys modern application development methodologies: Agile, DevOps, CI/CD, microservices, and serverless computing.
- Describes a proposed research methodology using literature review, case studies, comparative analysis, interviews/surveys, scenario simulations, data analysis, trend review, and expert validation.
- Main practical value for this vault is context for modern ERP/application delivery patterns, not ERP-specific evidence.
- Treat source cautiously: much of the extracted text describes proposed methodology and broad claims rather than reported empirical results.

## Details
The paper frames methodology choice as dependent on project requirements, scalability, flexibility, adaptability, and implementation ease. For enterprise integration, the relevant cross-links are DevOps and CI/CD for delivery pipelines, microservices for modular enterprise applications, and serverless for elastic/event-driven execution.
